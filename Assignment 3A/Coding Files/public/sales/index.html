Analysis for Brand Wise Automotive sales:

The economic car collection has a higher contribution in the sales. Maruti Suzuki being the most favoured and the most economically affordable brands, has the highest sales followed by Hyundai. Tata and Mahindra suffered a decrease in sales due to reliability issues. Kia and similar brands are fairly new to the marked and hence, the sales are gradually increasing for the same. Luxury automotive brands comparitively have lesser contribution to the net sale.
